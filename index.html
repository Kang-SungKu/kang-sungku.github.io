Research Topics

Integration of domain knowledge with data-driven methods

Click for more details!
In this research, a semantic method was developed that (1) utilizes natural language processing to extract the grammatical dependency graphs from textual rules, and then (2) utilizes a domain ontology to translate the dependency graphs into computer-understandable rules conforming to the manufacturing domain.



Control co-design via reinforcement learning

Click for more details!
I will extend my design automation method to incorporate machine learning control of floating offshore wind turbine blade pitches, another research project at Northeastern University I contributed, such that a wind turbine and its controller can be jointly optimized in a virtual environment.



Design automation for sustainability

Click for more details!
In this research, a virtual design environment inspired by Tetris AI, called MasonTris, is constructed where AI agents can explore simplified stone masonry designs by playing Tetris-like games.
